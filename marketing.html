Building Tiger Lane with care

Tiger Lane is an early-stage app created by Newat, a beginner developer focused on learning, improving, and delivering practical value to users.

🐅

About the Project

Tiger Lane is developed with attention, curiosity, and a strong desire to create something useful. As a beginner developer, Newat is actively learning from user feedback, improving the application step by step, and working to make the product clearer, smoother, and more valuable over time.

Independent development

Tiger Lane is built by an individual developer who is personally responsible for improving the app and supporting users.

Value-first mindset

The goal is to create a simple, helpful, and transparent experience before introducing any paid functionality.

Open communication

Users and Google Play reviewers can easily find privacy, support, and developer information on these linked pages.

Current Monetization Status

At this stage, Tiger Lane does not include paid features, subscriptions, or in-app purchases. The current priority is product quality, user trust, and learning how to make the application more useful.

Future Plans

In the future, paid features or subscriptions may be introduced to help support continued development and generate revenue. If monetization is added, it will be presented clearly inside the app and communicated transparently to users.

Commitment to Users

The developer is committed to improving Tiger Lane with care, honesty, and consistency. Feedback is welcome because it helps shape future updates and ensures the application grows in a useful direction.
