Privacy Policy

Tiger Lane is designed with a clear and simple privacy approach: no personal data collection, no storage, and no sharing with third parties.

Privacy Policy for Tiger Lane

Effective date: May 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tiger Lane, developed by Newat, handles information when users install or use the application.

Simple summary: Tiger Lane does not collect, store, transmit, sell, rent, or share any personal or non-personal user data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users. The app does not request, process, or store names, email addresses, location data, device identifiers, photos, contacts, payment information, or any other personal data.

Data Storage

We do not store user data on our servers or in any external database. Since no data is collected, there is no user data retained by Tiger Lane or the developer.

Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties. We do not sell, rent, exchange, or transfer user information to advertising networks, analytics providers, business partners, or other external services.

Analytics and Tracking

Tiger Lane does not use analytics tools, tracking technologies, advertising identifiers, cookies, or third-party SDKs for user tracking.

Children's Privacy

Because Tiger Lane does not collect any information, it does not knowingly collect personal data from children or any other users.

Security

We follow a data-minimization approach. Since the app does not collect or store user data, the risk of user data exposure is minimized.

Changes to This Policy

If Tiger Lane changes its data practices in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before any new data collection begins. Users and Google Play reviewers will be able to review the updated information on this page.
